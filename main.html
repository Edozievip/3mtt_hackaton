SAFETY TIPS

Here are some simple safety tips you should not overlook
  • Be Aware of Your Surroundings: Whether you’re walking in a parking lot or on the street, staying aware of your surroundings can help you avoid potential dangers.
  • Lock Your Doors and Windows: Always ensure that your doors and windows are locked, even when you're home.
  • Keep Emergency Contacts Handy: Save emergency numbers in your phone and have a physical list somewhere accessible at home.
  • Make Sure You Have a Working Fire Extinguisher in Your Home in case of any fire outbreak.
  • Don’t Share Too Much Online: Be cautious about sharing personal information on social media, as it can be used against you or lead to unwanted attention.
  • Know Your Exits: Familiarize yourself with the exits of any building you enter, including restaurants, theaters, and offices, in case of an emergency.
  • Avoid Distracted Driving: Put your phone away and minimize distractions while driving to reduce the risk of accidents.
  • Be Cautious with Personal Items: Keep your belongings close, especially in crowded places. Use a crossbody bag to deter pickpockets.
  • Use Proper Lighting: Ensure that your home and outdoor areas are well-lit, especially entryways, to deter intruders and make it safer to navigate at night.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If a situation feels off or uncomfortable, trust your instincts and remove yourself from it if possible
